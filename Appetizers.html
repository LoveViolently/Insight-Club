APPETIZERS

Lancashire hotpot is a traditional English casserole hailing from the heart of the North. This hearty dish features tender lamb or beef, slow-cooked with onions, carrots, and stock, all topped with thinly sliced potatoes that crisp up beautifully in the oven. Its rich, savory flavors make it a perfect dish for chilly nights and family gatherings.

Fish pie is a comforting, creamy dish perfect for cozy evenings. This classic British favorite combines a variety of fresh fish, such as cod, salmon, and prawns, in a rich, velvety white sauce, all topped with a golden, buttery mashed potato crust. It’s a warming meal that brings together simple ingredients for an indulgent, heartwarming experience.

Chicken Enchilada Casserole is a Tex-Mex dish made by layering tortillas, shredded chicken, enchilada sauce, cheese, and various seasonings, then baking it all together in a casserole dish. It’s a flavorful, cheesy, and easy-to-make meal with a bit of spice and a lot of comfort.

Fatteh is a Middle Eastern dish typically made with layers of crispy pita bread, rice, and a rich yogurt or tahini sauce, topped with chickpeas, nuts, and spices. It’s often garnished with fresh herbs like parsley and is a comforting, savory meal.
