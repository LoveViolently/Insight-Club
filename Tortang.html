TORTANG TALONG

Step 1: Grill the eggplant until the color of skin turns almost black. Let the eggplant cool for a while then peel off the skin. Set aside.

Step 2: Crack the eggs and place in a bowl. Add salt and beat the eggs.

Step 3: Place the eggplant on a flat surface and flatten using a fork. Dip the flattened eggplant in the beaten egg mixture 6. Heat the pan and pour the cooking oil.

Step 4: Fry the eggplant (that was dipped in the beaten mixture). Make sure that both sides are cooked. Frying time will take you about 3 to 4 minutes per side on medium heat.
